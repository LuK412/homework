Welcome and thank your for participating in the experiment.

In this experiment, participants are randomized into groups of three.
Within each group, participants are assigned one of three colors (red, blue, green).

All players are endowed with {{ Constants.endowment }}.

The red player will choose one of 6 possible payoff alterations which affect the payoffs of all participants in the same group. Blue and Green players are asked to indicate which allocation they would like red to choose. There is only one round in the experiment. Therefore every participant makes only one decision.

Below you can see the table of payoff changes. All players see the same table.

Advice has been formulated by other students about which option the red player should choose.
